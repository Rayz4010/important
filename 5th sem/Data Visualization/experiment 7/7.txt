Here’s a simple short version based on the PDF steps:

---

### **Power BI Report – Quick Steps**

1. **Open Power BI**
2. Click **Get Data → Excel/Text → Load File**
   (shown on page 1). 

---

### **Clean and Prepare Data**

3. Power Query opens → Click **Use First Row as Header**. 
4. Create any needed calculated column (example: attrition count).
5. Click **Close & Apply**. 

---

### **Create Visuals**

6. Start with a **KPI chart** (employee number). 
7. Add more visuals:

   * Pie Chart
   * Stacked Column Chart
   * Matrix
   * Donut Chart
   * Bar Chart
     (all shown through pages 5–9). 

---

### **Format Charts**

8. Use **Format Pane** to edit title, color, font, alignment (examples shown on pages 5–7). 

---

### **Sorting**

9. If sorting doesn't look right, create a sort column and sort visuals by it. 

---

### **Add Filters (Slicers)**

10. Add a **Slicer** to filter by category, age, or other fields. 

---

### **Final Dashboard**

11. Arrange visuals neatly to complete the report.

---

Done.
