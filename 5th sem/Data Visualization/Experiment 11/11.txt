### **i) Create KPIs**

Create calculated fields:

* **Employee Count:** `COUNT([Employee ID])`
* **Attrition Count:** `IF [Attrition] = "Yes" THEN 1 ELSE 0 END`
* **Attrition Rate:** `SUM([Attrition Count]) / COUNT([Employee ID])`
* **Active Employees:** `COUNT([Employee ID]) - SUM([Attrition Count])`
* **Average Age:** `AVG([Age])`

---

### **ii) Lollipop Chart (Attrition Rate by Gender)**

* Gender → Columns
* Attrition Rate → Rows
* Duplicate Attrition Rate
* First Marks: Bar (thin)
* Second Marks: Circle (large)
* Sync axes and show circle labels only.

### **iii) Pie Chart (Attrition by Department)**

### **iv) Bar Chart (Employees by Age Group)**
