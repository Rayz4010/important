Here’s the **short and simple** version of everything:

---

### **1) Import Data**

* Open Power BI → **Get Data**
* Load the **Sales dataset**

---

### **2) Choropleth (Filled) Map**

* Add **Filled Map**
* State → **Location**
* Revenue → **Values**
* Higher revenue shows darker color

---

### **3) Line Chart (Revenue by Month)**

* Add **Line Chart**
* Month → **Axis**
* Revenue → **Values**
* Sort months in order

---

### **4) Age Bin (Size = 10)**

* Modeling → **New Group**
* Field: **Age** → Bin Size: **10**
* Use a bar/column chart to show Revenue by Age Bin

---

### **5) Donut Chart (Revenue % by Region)**

* Add **Donut Chart**
* Region → Legend
* Revenue → Values
* Show **percentage labels**

(Zero axis measure created if required.)

---

### **6) Butterfly Chart (Male vs Female Revenue)**

* Create one bar chart for **Male**
* Duplicate and change filter to **Female**
* Reverse one chart’s axis
* Place both visuals side-by-side (mirror format)

---

### **7) Calculated Fields (Profitability)**

* Create:
  `AvgRevenuePerState = AVERAGE(Sales[Revenue])`
* Create:
  `Profitability = IF(SUM(Sales[Revenue]) > AvgRevenuePerState,"Profitable","Not Profitable")`
* Display using a map or table

---

### **8) Build the Dashboard**

* Arrange visuals neatly
* Add slicers (Month, Gender, Region, Category)

---

Done.
