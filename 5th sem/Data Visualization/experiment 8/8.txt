### **Power BI Filters — Quick Summary**

Filters help you show only the data you need.

There are **4 main filter types**:

1. **Visual Filter** → Affects only one chart.
2. **Page Filter** → Affects every visual on the current page.
3. **Report Filter** → Affects all pages in the report.
4. **Drill-through Filter** → Lets you right-click a value and open a detailed page for that item.



---

### **Applying Filters (Example with HR Dataset)**

#### **1) Department Filter**

* Drag **Department** → Filters pane
* Choose **Basic Filtering**
* Select only the values you want (Example: HR only).
  Now visuals show only HR results.


---

#### **2) Advanced Filter**

Goal: Show employees earning **≥ 10000**.

* Drag **Monthly Income** → Filters pane
* Change filter type → **Advanced filtering**
* Condition → **Greater than or equal to**
* Value → `10000`


---

#### **3) Top N Filter**

Goal: Show **Top 4 job roles with highest job satisfaction**.

* Select the visual
* In Filters pane → Job Role → choose **Top N**
* Enter **4**
* Choose value: **Sum of Job Satisfaction**
* Apply
